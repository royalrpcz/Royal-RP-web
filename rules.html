Server Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable gaming experience for everyone, please follow these rules.

01

Respectful Behavior

Treat all players with respect and dignity. Respectful behavior is the foundation of our community.

  • No insults or discrimination
  • No toxic behavior
  • No harassment of other players
02

Roleplay Rules

Realistic roleplay is the heart of our server. Follow the roleplay rules for an authentic experience.

  • Realistic behavior is mandatory
  • No powergaming or metagaming
  • Character development required
03

Communication

Proper communication is crucial for a good roleplay experience.

  • Local chat for in-character communication
  • OOC chat only for out-of-character communication
  • No spam or advertising
04

Vehicle Rules

Realistic driving and vehicle usage are important aspects of roleplay.

  • Follow traffic rules
  • No unrealistic driving
  • Park vehicles properly
05

Weapons and Combat

Weapons and combat actions must be realistic and justifiable.

  • Weapons only with appropriate license
  • Realistic combat actions
  • No RDM (Random Deathmatch)
06

General Rules

Additional important rules for a fair gaming experience.

  • No mods or cheats
  • No bug exploiting
  • Respect server administration

The team reserves the right to take appropriate action for rule violations.

Repeated violations may result in a permanent ban!
